Future Plans

As previously stated on my Personal Interests page, I spent a lot of time learning how to do hair. This includes hair color, haircuts, hair extensions, and making wigs. I have never been to cosmetology school, however for the past 10 years I have done my fair share of extensive training and research. I have shadowed hair stylists, taken online classes, watched videos, and repeatedly putting the information I have learned to the test by trying it myself over and over again until I was satisfied with the results. Unfortunately, in order to work in a salon I must go to cosmetology school, even though I have learned most of curriculum on my own. After college, I plan to get a job in the business industry in order to pay back student loans and go to cosmetology school part-time. After getting my cosmetology license, I plan to formulate and create my own brand of hair products that will be available to the general public and to salons, in addition to a unique hair color line specifically formulated to improve the health of a client's hair that will be available only to trained professional hair stylists.

These are just a few of the hair colors I have done.
