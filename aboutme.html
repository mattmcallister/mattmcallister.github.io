About Me

We lived in Alpharetta, Georgia for about 7 years, during which my younger brother, Ethan was born. When I was about 7, my family and I moved up to Wells, Maine for my Father's job, and to be closer to our relatives, as my mom is from New Jersey and my father is from Maine. Currently, I live with my mom in a different house in Wells, along with my 7-year-old dog named Teddy.
